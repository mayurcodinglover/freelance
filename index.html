Our Story

Born from Spice.
Shaped by Fire.

A small restaurant with a very specific point of view: that Indian cuisine, at its finest, is among the most complex and soulful food on earth.

Founded 2018

Chef Arjun Mehta's Vision

Arjun grew up in a household where cooking was conversation. His grandmother's kitchen in Jaipur was where he learned that a pinch of patience and a handful of spice could turn simple ingredients into memory.

After training at The Oberoi Centre of Learning in Delhi and a three-year stint at a Michelin-starred restaurant in Paris, he returned to India with a clear conviction: the greatest Indian food doesn't need to apologise to the world. It needs a stage worthy of it.

Spice & Ember opened in a converted merchant's haveli in Ahmedabad in 2018 with 42 covers, a hand-built tandoor, and a menu that changes every season.

What We Stand For

Three Commitments
We Don't Compromise

Authenticity

We don't chase trends. Our recipes are rooted in regional Indian traditions, adapted thoughtfully — never distorted for novelty. If a technique is hundreds of years old, there's probably a reason.

Sustainability

Seasonal menus aren't a marketing strategy for us — they're how we ensure our supply chain never outpaces our ethics. We compost, we minimise waste, and we pay our producers fairly.

Community

Every year, 10% of our profits go to the Ahmedabad Food Scholars programme, funding culinary education for young people from underserved communities across Gujarat.

Milestones

Our Journey So Far

2018

The First Flame

Spice & Ember opens its doors in a restored merchant's haveli in the old city of Ahmedabad. 42 covers. One tandoor. A menu of twelve dishes.

2020

National Recognition

Named "Best New Fine Dining Restaurant" at the India Food Awards. Chef Arjun appears on the cover of Gourmet India magazine. The waiting list hits three weeks.

2022

The Private Dining Room

We open a ten-cover private dining space for bespoke events. The tasting menu — seven courses, wine pairing, personal interaction with the chef — launches to immediate acclaim.

2024

Michelin Plate Recognition

Spice & Ember receives a Michelin Plate — an acknowledgment of good cooking at every visit. The recognition cements our place among India's finest regional dining destinations.
